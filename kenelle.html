Meet Kenelle

Why are you taking this class?

I am taking this class in order to change careers after working as a dental assistant for 12 years. This career is challenging and rewarding at the same time and requires consistent learning. I really like that about the tech field as I feel I could never get bored.

What are some of your hobbies?

I am a resin artist in my spare time and I love to listen to music.

What are some of your long-term goals?

My long-term goals include working as a hybrid software engineer/ux-ui designer, landing a six figure job, buying a house and helping other people get into the tech field in order to make a better life for them and their families.

What do you have in common?

We all want to become more involved with tech and develop the skills to navigate the various paths that front end development can provide. We all also want to be able to help others enter the tech field.
